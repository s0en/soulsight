Kai’s Journey

Follow Kai as he grows from a shy young boy into a confident explorer by harnessing the gifts of his figures.

Age 6: The Discovery

On his sixth birthday, Kai discovers a mysterious box from his late grandfather containing ten wooden figures. Each one radiates a unique warmth and glow, sparking his imagination.

Age 7–11: The Lessons

As Kai grows, the figures teach him to be brave, creative, caring and mindful. He learns how Archie fosters courage, Piper sparks creativity, Cora nurtures kindness and so on — each power guiding him through school, friendships and family life.

Age 11–12: The Awakening

By early adolescence, Kai realizes that the magic was inside him all along. With confidence and clarity, he begins to share his learnings with others and becomes a guiding light for his friends.

Your SoulSight Journey

Embark on your own adventure with practices inspired by Kai’s experiences. Each phase helps children (and parents) find clarity and strength.

Balance & Play

Develop a steady rhythm of rest and exploration. Create a playful “me time” every day, balance learning with fun and let imagination lead the way.

SETH Keys

Safety, Empathy, Trust and Harmony. Practice listening, share your feelings and build safe spaces where everyone can express themselves freely.

Mastery & Growth

Put your newfound skills to the test. Try solving real-life challenges together, reflect on successes and discover new powers along the way.
